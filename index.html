BentoBox

By ScrubTier

The World Bestest Arcade Pad

Small and Sturdy

Solid as a rock

The BentoBox is designed to be portable and durable.

Being small enough to fit into your pocket and take with you anywhere, this arcade pad means that you're always ready for a fight!

Mechanical

Buttery Smooth

Built using only Kailh® Low Profile switches the BentoBox provides the same crisp feedback and satisfying feel of your mechanical keyboard.

The ergonomic layout allows you to always able to react in time and the low travel distance means that your combos will be easier to land and your blocks will be harder to break, and with only 1.3mm of movement before the actuation point your strikes with be lightning fast.

This is the part where I'd love to talk about the different switches and their different properties but I don't know what colour we're using, or if there's any customization available

This arcade pad also features an overclocked 1000hz polling rate and an input latency of under 1ms.

We know that when it comes to mechanical switches, not everyone can agree. That's why the BentoBox has standard 3 pin connectors, so you can use any 3pin switch you like!

Unfortunately you won't be able to blame the controller when you lose anymore.

Combining Style with Substance

Free Style Points

The BentoBox comes with RGB LEDs carefully placed all over the arcade pad. These LEDs make you stand out from the crowd, and will full onboard customization there's always a way to make your style unique.

With full onboard customization, powered by the included Raspberry Pi® Zero, you'll find setting your new lighting a breeze, and easy to change on the fly.

Future Proof

Full Power, any Place

Compatible with Windows, Switch, Android, Steam Deck, PS3 and PS4

No Cable? No Problem.

The BentoBox connects via USB-C cable (sold separately), so if you ever forget your cable, or a little bit of, shall we say 'excitement'?, renders your cable inoperable you can use any USB-C cable and you'll be back in the action.

Even More Style Points

The BentoBox's top plate can be easily removed and replaced, combining with the RGB LEDs your BentoBox will always look the part.

And it's all open-source

We don't keep things locked away

All of the programming that goes into making the BentoBox work is entirely open source. Based on the GP2040 Project, this arcade pad is truly customizable from the ground up, and from the inside out.
